Know details of Surya Mandir

Where is Surya Mandir located in Jamshedpur

Surya Mandir is located in the Tata Steel Colony (TISCO Colony) of Sidhgora in the steel city Jamshedpur.

How is the architecture of Surya Mandir, Jamshedpur?

Surya Mandir is a Hindu temple dedicated to the Sun God. The temple depicts a chariot that is harnessed by seven white horses. In Hindu Mythology, Lord Surya or the Sun Lord is portrayed as riding a chariot harnessed by seven horses which personify seven colors of light which are Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, and Violet. The horses also represent the seven days of the week and are named after the seven meters of Sanskrit Prosody as Gayatri, Brihati, Ushnih, Jagati, Trishtubha, Anushtubha and Pankti.

Does Surya Mandir Park, Jamshedpur have a boating facility?

Boating was one of the major attractions of the park but lately, it is not operational.

What is Surya Mandir, Jamshedpur famous for?

Surya Mandir is famous for the grand celebration of Chhath. Chhath Puja is one of the main festivals celebrated in the temple campus. Devotees from the entire city and the nearby cities and villages visit the temple to offer prayers to Lord Surya. Every year on the evening of the first day of the puja, the temple authorities invite famous singers of the country to perform bhajans.
